Admissions Open for 2024-25

Admissions

At Kamlapur Govt. Higher Secondary School, we welcome students who are eager to learn and grow. Below are the details for the admission process for the 2024-25 academic year.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum age for admission to Class 1: 6 years as on 1st April 2024.
  • Admission to higher classes is based on the availability of seats and performance in the entrance test.
  • Students from recognized schools are eligible to apply for transfer admissions.

Admission Process

  1. Collect the admission form from the school office or download it from the school website.
  2. Submit the filled form along with the required documents to the school office.
  3. Appear for the entrance test (if applicable).
  4. Selected students will be notified via phone or email.
  5. Complete the fee payment process to confirm admission.

Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate
  • Previous School Leaving Certificate (if applicable)
  • Marksheet of the last academic year
  • Passport-sized photographs (4)
  • Residential proof (e.g., Aadhar Card)

Important Dates

Form Distribution: 1st January 2024 to 31st January 2024

Last Date for Submission: 5th February 2024

Entrance Test: 15th February 2024

Result Declaration: 20th February 2024
